Dive Deep Into Beats

Music is an art form and cultural activity whose medium is sound organized in time. General definitions of music include common elements such as pitch (which governs melody and harmony), rhythm (and its associated concepts tempo, meter, and articulation), dynamics (loudness and softness), and the sonic qualities of timbre and texture (which are sometimes termed the "color" of a musical sound).

Music Genre

Alternative

Alternative music was a phrase invented in early 80s describing bands which broke from the barrage of pop and hair metal and formed a new direction of more focused and honest rock.

Anime

Music and Japanese anime have always been intertwined. The intro and end credits are always accompanied by a song.

Blues

Blues is a music genre and musical form which was originated in the Deep South of the United States.

Children's Music

Children's music or kids' music is music composed and performed for children.

Classical

Classical music is a tricky genre, mainly because to the uninformed everything before jazz sounds like it!

Dance

Dance music is music composed specifically to facilitate or accompany dancing.

Electronic

Electronic music is music that employs electronic musical instruments, digital instruments and circuitry-based music technology.

Fitness

The interplay of exercise and music have been long-discussed, crossing the disciplines of biomechanics, neurology, physiology, and sport psychology.

Hip-Hop

Hip hop or hip-hop, is a culture and art movement that began in the Bronx in New York City during the early 1970s.

Jazz

Jazz is a music genre that originated in the African-American communities of New Orleans, United States.

Pop

Pop music, often called simply pop, is contemporary music and a common type of popular music.

Rock

Rock music is a broad genre of popular music that originated as "rock and roll" in the United States in the early 1950s.
